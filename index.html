Unfrozen Ground, Murky Waters

Welcome to this short comic, created in collaboration with local artist Élise of Cambridge Bay (Ikaluktutiak). Through the eyes of young fishers and curious minds, it shares how the land is changing with permafrost thaw — and how we learn together, from both science and experience.

Contact & Acknowledgments

This comic was created as part of a MSc research project (Loek Pascaud) and outreach initiative led by the Université de Sherbrooke, in collaboration with local partners in Ikaluktutiak (Cambridge Bay), Nunavut.
Illustrations by Élise Imbeau.

About mee

Hi! I'm Loek — a master's student at Université de Sherbrooke working on permafrost & lakes in the Canadian Arctic, and really interested in community-based science and local knowledge. This comic is part of an effort to connect research with art and storytelling. For questions or any comments, feel free to reach out: loek.pascaud@usherbrooke.ca
